About Vizag Steel Plant

Located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, Vizag Steel Plant is one of the leading steel manufacturers in India, providing quality steel products for various industries.

Our Products

We manufacture a wide range of steel products, including bars, billets, and coils, catering to construction, automotive, and other industries.

Sustainability Initiatives

We are committed to sustainable practices, with initiatives for reducing carbon emissions, waste management, and energy efficiency.

Our Future Vision

Vizag Steel Plant aims to expand its operations and enhance its technological capabilities to become a global leader in steel manufacturing.

Our History

1982

Vizag Steel Plant was established to meet the growing demand for steel in India.

1992

Production of high-quality steel products began, expanding the plant’s production capacity.

2000

Introduction of new technologies to improve the plant’s sustainability and efficiency.

Present

Vizag Steel Plant is a leading supplier of quality steel to national and international markets.
